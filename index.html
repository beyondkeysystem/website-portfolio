About

Greg Barnett is a Full-Stack Web Developer, and guitarist currently residing in Long Beach NY. Graduating SUNY ONEONTA in 2009 with a major in Music Industry & a minor in Guitar Performance, Greg is a well-seasoned professional in the music industry as well as an excellent guitarist.

In 2012, Greg decided to return to school to pursue a degree in his other passion: Information Technology. Graduating in 2013, Greg emerged from school a well rounded technologist equipped with expertise in programing, networking, and database systems. Greg is currently working at the Harry Fox Agency (HFA) as a Full-Stack Web Developer where he gets to combine his two passions, music and technology, to build software that helps music publishers and song writers in the music industry.
