Greg Barnett
Full-Stack Web Developer & Guitarist
About

Greg Barnett is a Full-Stack Web Developer, and Guitarist currently residing in Long Beach NY. Graduating SUNY ONEONTA in 2009 with a major in Music Industry & a minor in Guitar Performance, Greg is well-seasoned professional in the music industry as well as an execellent guitarist. In 2012 Greg decided to return to school to pursue a degree in his other passsion, Information Technology. Graduating in 2013 Greg emerged from school a well rounded technologist with expertise in programing, networking, and database systems. Greg is currently working at the Harry Fox Agency (HFA) as a Full-Stack Web Developer, where he gets to combine his two passions music & technology to build software that help's music publishers & song writers in the music industry.
